apps:

We made an app for Coop Extra:

With the ”Camp Extra” app, Extra staff can complete all mandatory training and receive weekly challenges in the training course.

In Camp Extra you can: get information about everything new and exciting happening in Extra, build your own avatar, compete against colleagues, look up important reference works, access this week's and next week's customer newspaper, and more.

We made an app for Morgenlevering:

With the ”Morgenlevering” app: freshly baked bread, newspapers, freshly squeezed juice and fresh baked goods are at your fingertips! Order for the family or as a gift for someone you love.

Morgenlevering delivers to Eastern Norway, Bergen & Hordaland, Trondheim, Stavanger and Southern Rogaland, Haugalandet, Ryfylke and Hardanger.

You can choose between regular one-time delivery or subscription to your regular favorites.

We made an app for Sykehuset Østfold:

”Veileder i akuttpsykiatri” (VAP) is used by doctors and other health professionals at psychiatric wards in Norway, in somatic departments, emergency services and for first-line service employees.

VAP helps facilitate the training of new doctors and other health professionals.
